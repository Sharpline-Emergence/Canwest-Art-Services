Privacy Policy

Canwest Art Services is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) of British Columbia.

1. Information we collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us — for example, when you submit a contact form, request a consultation, or make a purchase through our Etsy store. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and details about artwork you wish to have appraised or restored.

2. How we use your information

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry, fulfill your request, or provide the service you have requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Contact forms

Contact and booking forms on this website are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a secure third-party form service. Submissions are transmitted securely and delivered to our business email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Online purchases

Purchases of art cards, paintings, and other products are processed through our Etsy store (etsy.com/shop/BrimacombeStudio). All payment and transaction data is handled by Etsy and subject to Etsy's privacy policy. Canwest Art Services does not collect or store payment information.

5. Cookies & analytics

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or analytics software. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may request that your information be deleted at any time by contacting us.

7. Your rights

Under BC's PIPA, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, request corrections to that information, and withdraw consent to its use. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us directly:
