Professional fine art services

Appraisals & Restorations

Canwest Art provides a variety of art-related services for both individual and corporate clients. With over 40 years of experience, our services include fine art and antique appraisals for insurance and estate purposes, as well as market evaluations. Free of charge, we are willing to offer our opinion as to whether your family heirloom or second-hand store “treasure” is worth appraising.

Canwest Art also provides services for the cleaning and restoration of paintings in all mediums, as well as for damaged frames, sculpture, and antiques. We are happy to provide fine art consultations for new or experienced collectors. We also provide corporate fine art and artisanal gifts, custom framing, and art classes in watercolours, oils and acrylics. Please feel free to contact us for prices and quotes.

Art appraisals

How it works

From first call to finished work

1. Free initial consultation

Contact us to describe your piece and your needs. We’ll advise on next steps at no charge — in person, by phone, or by email.

2. Free pickup & delivery

We offer complimentary pickup and delivery throughout the Lower Mainland, from Vancouver to Hope.

3. Written report or restoration

Appraisals are delivered as a formal written report. Restorations are fully documented with before-and-after photography.
