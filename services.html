Professional fine art services

Appraisals & Restorations

With decades of experience in fine art valuation and conservation, Canwest Art Services provides trusted appraisal and restoration services to private collectors, estates, and corporate clients across the Lower Mainland. We offer a no-fee initial consultation and free pickup and delivery from Vancouver to Hope.

Art appraisals

Certified fine art appraisals for insurance, estate, donation, or resale purposes. We provide written documentation accepted by major Canadian insurance carriers and financial institutions.

  • Insurance appraisals
  • Estate & probate appraisals
  • Charitable donation appraisals
  • Resale valuation
  • Corporate art collection appraisals

Art restorations

Professional conservation and restoration of oils, acrylics, and works on paper. All work is reversible and fully documented — we treat every piece with the same care we’d give our own.

  • Canvas repair & relining
  • Cleaning & varnish removal
  • Inpainting & retouching
  • Frame conservation
  • Works on paper conservation

How it works

From first call to finished work

1. Free initial consultation

Contact us to describe your piece and your needs. We’ll advise on next steps at no charge — in person, by phone, or by email.

2. Free pickup & delivery

We offer complimentary pickup and delivery throughout the Lower Mainland, from Vancouver to Hope.

3. Written report or restoration

Appraisals are delivered as a formal written report. Restorations are fully documented with before-and-after photography.
