About Cranfield Colours

A legacy of professional-grade colour

Cranfield Colours has been producing artists’ materials in Britain since 1881. Their Artists’ Oils are formulated with the highest-grade pigments and cold-pressed linseed oil, delivering exceptional permanence, consistency, and handling. As an authorized BC distributor, we carry both Artists’ Oils and Studio Oils lines.
